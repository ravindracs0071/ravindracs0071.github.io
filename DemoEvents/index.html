Dear Colleagues,
Greetings from the holy city of Varanasi and from the Organizing Committee of ISACON-UP 2020.

It gives us immense pleasure to invite all of you for the Annual State Conference of the Indian Society of Anaesthesiologists-Uttar Pradesh 2020) scheduled from 2nd to 4th October 2020 at Swatantrata Bhawan, Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi, the spiritual capital of India.

ISACONUP-2020 will give all of you a golden opportunity to empower yourself by learning, sharing experiences and meeting with stalwarts of Anaesthesia. The conference will feature Workshops, Lectures, Scientific sessions, Quiz contest and exhibitions.

We promise you excellent academic interactions, with a host of eminent international and national faculty who will share their knowledge and experience on recent advances in the field of Anaesthesia.

Thus, along with a good academic feast you all will have an excellent religious feast as well and you will reach back to your home with much enlightened mind.

Ensuring you a very good hospitality as Varanasi is famous for that, catering lacs of tourist every year.
With warm regards

Prof. S K Mathur
