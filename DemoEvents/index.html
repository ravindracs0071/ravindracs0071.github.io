Welcome to ISACONUP 2020

Greetings from the holy city of Varanasi and from the Organizing Committee of ISACON-UP 2020.

It gives us immense pleasure to invite all of you for the 42th Annual State Conference of the Indian Society of Anaesthesiologists-Uttar Pradesh 2020) scheduled from 2nd to 4th October 2020 at Swatantrata Bhawan, Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi, the spiritual capital of India.

We promise you an exciting and informative scientific program that will be broad varied, encompassing plenary lectures, breakout sessions & workshops by renowned national experts.

The program will feature streams with a wide variety of topics, taking into consideration our Conference theme 'Anaesthesia': An amalgamation of Diverse specialities" enabling you to tailor the programme specifically to your own interest & clinical practice, as well as information packed for pre conference workshops on 2nd October 2020.

Thus, along with a good academic feast you all will have an excellent religious feast as well and you will reach back to your home with much enlightened mind.

Ensuring you a very good hospitality as Varanasi is famous for that, catering lacs of tourist every year.

Long Live ISA !
