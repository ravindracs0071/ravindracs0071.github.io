SPONSORSHIP & EXHIBITION CONDITIONS:

The ISACONUP 2020 Organising Committee reserves the right to accept or reject any application for sponsorship at its absolute discretion. Company advertisements and satchel inserts are subject to approval from the Conference Organising Committee.

All sponsorship / exhibition packages are exclusive of GST as applicable.

Payment Terms: To secure a pencil booking, a non-refundable deposit of 10 % must be paid within 15 working days of a pencil booking being agreed. The remaining 50% will be invoiced in September 2019 and must be paid in full no later than 16th December 2019.

To Confirm Sponsorship: Should you wish to confirm sponsorship, please return the completed Acceptance Form to Prof. (Dr.) Pushkar Ranjan or you can email at ISACONUP2020@gmail.com . On receipt of the deposit payment, a confirmation letter will be given.

Allocation: Sponsorship and exhibition packages, which may be limited in number, will be generally allocated to those organisations / companies who apply earliest. Allocation of sponsorship packages and stalls is at the discretion of the ISACONUP 2020 Organising Committee whose decision will be final.

Cancellation Policy: Once an Acceptance Form has been received, any cancellation must be advised in writing to ISACONUP 2020 Organising Committee. If the cancellation is received up to 2 months before the event an administration fee of 75% of the total amount is retained. If the cancellation is received less than 1 month prior, no refund is applicable.

Exceptional Circumstances: The ISACONUP 2020 Organising Committee reserves the right to change the venue and duration if exceptional circumstances demand. In the event of a change of venue and/or duration, the agreement to participate will remain in force as long as the sponsor / exhibitor is informed at least one month before the planned conference.

The sponsor / exhibitor will acknowledge that the ISACONUP 2020 Organising Committee and venue will not be responsible for any loss or damage to the sponsor’s / exhibitor’s property and that all exhibitor material and equipment is the sole responsibility of the exhibitor. The sponsor / exhibitor agrees to participate at its own risk.

Venue: The sponsor / exhibitor and invitees of the sponsor / exhibitor are also to observe the rules, regulations and procedures as prescribed by the venue.
