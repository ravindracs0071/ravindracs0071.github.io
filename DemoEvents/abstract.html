Abstract

On behalf of the Scientific Committee of 12th Annual Conference of the Indian Association of Paediatric Anaesthesiologists, we invite you to submit abstract of your Poster Presentations.

Note: Request you to please read the guidelines given below for submitting your abstract. For any further information please feel free to contact us on isaconup2020@gmail.com

Points to be Noted

In the online abstract submission system, please use the latest version of the following web browsers; (Internet Explorer, Firefox, Safari and Google Chrome)
One author cannot submit more than 3 posters.
After submitting your abstract, please be sure to check whether it has been submitted successfully and please note the Auto Generated Submission ID for future.
Registration for the conference is mandatory for considering the abstract by the scientific committee.
Only the presenting author will be allowed to present his/her work, under any circumstances Co-Author will not be allowed to present the work.
The decision of the scientific committee is final.

Guidelines for Abstract Submission

  • Registration for ISACONUP 2020 is a prerequisite for any presentation.
  • Eligibility- The abstract must be original and should not be published or presented elsewhere earlier.
  • There will be only Poster presentation.
  • All the abstracts will be peer reviewed and the final decision to allocate an abstract to any type of presentation rests with the Scientific Committee.
  • However, acceptance will be notified only after peer review and revision, with in 3 weeks of submission. Rights of acceptance/ rejection rests with the scientific committee and it is final.
  • Last date for submission of abstract is 31 December 2019.
  • The authors can modify their abstract till last date and beyond this, no modifications in abstract will be allowed.
  • A maximum 4 Authors only is allowed for each abstract (including the presenting author) and if there are more than 4 authors, only the names of first 4 authors will be published and remaining names will be automatically eliminated.
  • Abstracts to be submitted online at www.isaconup2020.com
  • All submissions are online through website only, submission of offline soft copy/ submission by mail or hard copy submissions are not accepted under any circumstance.
  • Presentation of paper is by the first author only. 2 or 3 authors will be allowed to present only under extra ordinary circumstances, with prior permission of the organizing scientific committee chairperson.
  • The presenting author’s name must be highlighted and the presenting author’s full name, institution, address for communication, phone number and email address must be provided. Email address is mandatory as all further communications will be done via email.
  • Abstracts stating "data will be discussed in presentation" will not be accepted.
  • Abstracts will be published in the official publication of IAPACON 2020 and a copy of the Declaration of consent for publication should be signed and sent with the abstract.
  • The presenting author is required to ensure that all co-authors are aware of the contents of the abstract before submission.
  • The abstracts submitted will be sent for peer review. Such abstracts requiring corrections as per peer reviewers comment should be revised and resubmitted within 5-7 days failing which the abstracts will be rejected. All revised papers should submit through the 'submit Revised Paper' button.

Instructions for Poster Presentation

  • Each poster session author will be provided with a 4 feet height X 3 feet width poster board area and mounting pins. Keep the size of your poster preferably up to 3.75 feet height X 2.75 feet width (Portrait Orientation).
  • The board will indicate the poster number in upper right or left corner.
  • All authors are requested to display their posters by TIME on DATE and have to be removed before TIME on DATE. (will inform before conference)
  • Evaluation of Posters shall be done on DATE at TIME. (will inform before conference)
  • Each poster must include text in a large enough font (~24 point font) to be read easily by attendees from a distance of 4 to 5 feet or more. Lettering on illustrations should be large and legible. Photographs should be a minimum of 5 x 7 inches.
  • The poster must have a short Title, name of presenting author and co-authors, name and address of the institute along with email address for correspondence.
  • The following format must be followed:
    1. Background
    2. Aims
    3. Materials & Methods
    4. Results
    5. Conclusions
  • Avoid overcrowding figures and cramming too many numbers into tables. Legends and titles should accompany all figures, tables, photographs, etc. in order to allow their immediate identification.
  • Please remember that it is very important that you prepare your poster BEFORE you travel to conference venue and that you bring it yourself.
  • Registration for conference is one of the pre-requisites for abstract submission. If you have not registered yet, please register through conference website isaconup2020.com
  • Responsibility for Poster Presentation: The Organizing Committee does not assume any responsibility for material displayed on the poster boards & will not be able to take care of your expenses towards attending ISACONUP 2020 viz travel, accommodation & registration.
  • No-show policy: ISACONUP 2020 considers that submission of an abstract constitutes a commitment by the author to present the abstract. Therefore ISACONUP 2020 expects you to present your poster at the Conference. If you are absolutely not able to present your poster you must inform us by email at earliest.
