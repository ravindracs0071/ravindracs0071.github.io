IAPACON 2020 - Online Registration Portal

Please read the guidelines before filling up your online application.

Regsitration Guidlines/Instructions:

  • First time users, kindly select I am a new user and enter your desired Email ID and Password, Please note this registered email id will be used for all future communications.
  • If you are a Pre-registered delegate, kindly select Already Registered User and login with your registered email id and password.
  • If you do not remember your password, please select forgotten your password then enter your registered email to get the password reset link.
  • Please fill in authenticated personal details, as per your Passport (International Delegate) / Aadhar card (Indian Delegate)
  • Please cross check your details before proceeding everytime.
  • To edit your details click on Edit option/My account/Edit profile.
  • Fees should be paid in INR/USD only.

Terms & Conditions:

  • Registration fee will be based on the date of receipt of payment.
  • Delegate will receive payment confirmation mail after successful registration.
  • Cheque will not be accepted for spot registration.
  • Registration is compulsory for paper publication.
  • Only registered delegates will be allowed to attend the conference and receive conference kit.
  • Accompanying Person fees includes access to Banquet dinner only.
  • No kit guarantee for spot registration.
  • No conference kit will be provided to accompanying person.
  • Limited number of registrations are available for each workshop.
  • If paying through bank transfer kindly send a scan copy of Bank advice/Transaction receipt to below email address to track the payment.
  • Students must submit a letter/NOC certificate from their Head of the Department/Institute to register for the conference.

Cancellation and Refund Policy

  • Request for cancellation for refund must be made in writing through mail.
  • 50% refund will be made before 1st Dec-2019.
  • Refund will be reimbursed by cheque only after completion of the conference.
